What it does

Everything you need on track

One app to time, read the engine and follow the line.

⏱️

F1-style timing

Purple/green/yellow sectors with history+ghost benchmark, theoretical lap and live delta. Finish line and splits on the map.

📡

OBD-II Telemetry

Connect an ELM327 adapter via Wi-Fi, classic Bluetooth or BLE. RPM, speed, throttle, temperatures, error codes (DTC).

🗺️

Maps & routes

Standard, satellite or terrain maps. Draw routes, record driving (Tour) or import GPX and Google Maps links.

📊

Custom HUD

Configurable grid dashboard: speed, revs, times, temps, G-force, minimap. Auto-merge adjacent cells.

🏁

Live Dashboard

Lap, delta, F1 sectors, rotating minimap, rally corner indicator, G-forces and music — all at a glance.

🌀

G-Forces

Real-time lateral and longitudinal accelerations from the phone's sensors, visible in HUD.

🚨

Personal speed traps

Place your targets on the map. Always-on GPS monitoring and separate records per car.

🏎️

Vehicles & comparison

Built-in make and model catalog. Compare the best times and sectors between your cars on the same track.

🎵

Music control

Play, pause and next track of any system player, directly from the HUD without leaving the app.

📸

Social highlights

Create vertical stories with your photos or videos, sector times and F1 colors to share on social media.

📂

History & analysis

Saved sessions, lap-time and speed-trace graphs, sector list. Export CSV, GPX (Premium: VBO/NMEA).

🔒

Total Privacy

No accounts, no tracking, no ads. Your data stays on your phone. No servers of ours.

Starting a lap

Start how you want

Three modes to start the timer perfectly.

MANUAL
Now

Press Start and the time begins instantly. The lap closes when you cross the finish line again.

LIGHTS
On green

Starting grid style: the timer starts exactly when the lights go out.

AUTOMATIC
At the line

Arms automatically near the line: the time starts the moment you cross the start line.

Transparency

Transparent permissions

Every permission has one purpose: making the app work locally. Here's exactly what's needed and why.

📍

GPS Location

To time laps, record your trajectory and show the map. Background use lets a session keep timing even when the screen is off.

📶

Bluetooth

To connect to the OBD-II adapter (ELM327) via classic Bluetooth or BLE to read telemetry.

🌐

Internet

Exclusively to download map tiles (OpenStreetMap/Esri). No personal data is sent to our servers.

📳

Motion sensors

To measure and display G-forces in real time. Data stays on-device.

🔔

Notifications

For app alerts, such as showing that the service is active during a background session.

🎧

Notification access

(Android only, optional) To read the active track title and show you controls. We do not read message contents.

📸

Camera & photos

To record a video or pick a photo to use as a background for a highlight to share. Files are not transmitted to us.

📡

Local Network

(iOS only) To connect to Wi-Fi OBD-II adapters on the local network.

🎵

Apple Music

(iOS only) To show the current track and control music playback while driving.

Privacy

Your data stays yours

Razorline does not collect, sell or share personal data. Location, Bluetooth and sensors are only used to make the app work on your device: laps, sessions and records are saved exclusively locally.

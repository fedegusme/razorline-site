Privacy Policy

Razorline · Last update: July 14, 2026 · Developer: Federico Gusmeroli
In short: Razorline does not collect personal data, does not sell it, and does not share it. No accounts, no ads, no tracking. Everything you create stays on your device.

1. What data we process

Razorline works locally. The data you generate — sessions and lap times, GPS traces, tracks and routes, vehicles, records and settings — is stored exclusively on your device. It is not sent to us or to any server we own (we have no servers). We do not ask for your name, email, phone number or any identifying data.

2. Transparent permissions and why they are needed

Every permission has one purpose: making the app work locally. Here's exactly what's needed and why.

📍

GPS Location

To time laps, record your trajectory and show the map. Background use lets a session keep timing even when the screen is off. Location is processed on the device and is not transmitted to us.

📶

Bluetooth

To connect to the OBD-II adapter (ELM327) via classic Bluetooth or BLE to read telemetry.

🌐

Internet

Exclusively to download map tiles (OpenStreetMap/Esri). No personal data is sent to our servers.

📳

Motion sensors

To measure and display G-forces in real time. Data stays on-device.

🔔

Notifications

For app alerts, such as showing that the service is active during a background session.

🎧

Notification access

(Android only, optional) To read the active track title and show you controls. We do not read or transmit the content of your notifications.

📸

Camera & Media

Used solely locally to record a video (with audio) or pick a photo as the background when creating a social highlight ("Story"). We do not transmit files to us.

📡

Local Network

(iOS only) To connect to Wi-Fi OBD-II adapters on the local network.

🎵

Apple Music

(iOS only) To show the current track and control music playback while driving.

🔋

Keep Screen On

Wakelock to prevent the screen from turning off while driving in the HUD or Tours.

3. Third-party services

To display maps, the app requests map tiles from external providers (e.g. OpenStreetMap). Those servers receive standard network requests (such as the device's IP address and the coordinates of the displayed area). We do not receive or retain this data. See: OpenStreetMap Privacy Policy.

4. Subscriptions and In-App Purchases (Premium)

Razorline offers advanced features through a "Premium" subscription. Payments are handled exclusively and securely by the Apple App Store and Google Play Store. We do not collect, process, or store your financial data or credit cards in any way. Subscription status is verified by querying the store. A minimal encrypted subscription-verification cache may remain in the iOS Keychain or Android secure storage to ensure offline functionality (grace period). Apple and Google retain transaction records under their legal and anti-fraud duties.

5. Data sharing and selling

We do not share or sell any data. If you choose to export or share a file (GPX, CSV or a "challenge"), that action is started voluntarily by you through your device's share menu.

6. Retention and deletion

Your data stays on your device until you delete it in the app or uninstall Razorline. Uninstalling the app removes the local data.

7. Children

The app is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their data.

8. Safe use

Razorline is intended for closed circuits, track days or private areas. Do not use it on public roads and do not interact with the phone while driving.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. The date at the top shows the latest version.

10. Contact

For privacy questions: support.razorline@gmail.com
